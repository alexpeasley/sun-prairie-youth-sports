‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Which Football/Basketball board do I join?

Sun Prairie is divided by the High School Attendance boundaries. If your child will eventually attend SP East, join the Cardinal programs. If SP West, join the Wolves programs.

Are there scholarships available?

Yes. Most organizations (SPYBAS, SPSC, SYBA) offer financial aid or registration fee waivers. Contact the board treasurer via their official websites for confidential assistance.

When do tryouts usually happen?

Travel/Competitive tryouts for summer sports (Baseball/Softball) usually happen in late July. For winter sports (Basketball/Hockey), they happen in September/October.
